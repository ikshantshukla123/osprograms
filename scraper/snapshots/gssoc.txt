Timeline
What & When
Key milestones for GirlScript Summer of Code 2026
01
24th March 2026
Launch Event + Applications Open
GSSoC 2026 kicks off live! Attend the launch event for a walkthrough of the program, track guide, and the Cloudinary bonus session.
02
End of March / Early April 2026
Selections
Applications are reviewed and participants are selected. Contributors, mentors, project admins, and ambassadors are confirmed for the program.
03
April 2026
Onboarding and Kick-off
Selected participants are onboarded. Projects go live, contributors pick their issues, mentors are assigned, and the building begins.
04
May to June 2026
Contribution Period
The main program period. Contributors work on projects, submit PRs, build agents, earn points, and climb the leaderboard.
05
June to July 2026
Final Evaluations and Results
All contributions are evaluated, leaderboards are finalized, and top contributors across both tracks are recognized and rewarded.

Scoring & Rewards
Leaderboard
& Rewards
Every contribution counts. The leaderboard tracks your progress in real time, earn points, climb ranks, and get recognized at the end of the program.
01
Contribution-based scoring
Every merged PR, deployed agent, and completed task earns points on the live leaderboard.
02
Bonus points via partners
Partner bounty programs (Cloudinary & more) unlock extra leaderboard points on top of your contributions.
03
Recognition for top contributors
Certificates, swag, and featured recognition for the top performers across both tracks at program end.
