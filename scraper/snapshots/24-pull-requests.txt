About 24 Pull Requests
24 Pull Requests' goal is to encourage contribution to open source projects during December. The site suggests open projects, highlights tickets that are good for new contributors, provides
guides for contributing
and promotes good contributions submitted each day.
Giving back to open source.
Open source software has probably made you more productive this year. Let's give something back.
The idea is simple: 'Send 24 pull requests between December 1st and December 24th', encouraging contributors to give back to open source projects with little gifts of code throughout December.
